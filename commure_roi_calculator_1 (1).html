How we calculate your estimate
Abandonment recovery
Applies Terra Bella's 45% abandonment reduction to your monthly call volume, then multiplies recovered calls by your booking conversion rate and average appointment value.
Conversion lift
Applies Terra Bella's 20% booking conversion improvement to your connected call volume (calls that reach an agent), calculating incremental appointments and associated revenue.
Operational efficiency
Estimates FTE redeployment based on call volume automation. Patient Intake Agent typically automates 25–40% of routine scheduling tasks, reducing manual handle time per call.
Results are estimates based on Terra Bella Health outcomes and industry benchmarks. Actual results will vary by health system size, EHR environment, call mix, and implementation scope. All calculations use operational inputs only — no patient data is collected or processed. HIPAA compliant.
